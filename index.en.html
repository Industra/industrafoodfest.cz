Look forward to the last Saturday of the summer holidays. A festival with lots of honest fresh food with a lovely accompanying program and a great feel is comming! On 4000 m2 selected professionals and amateurs will offer street food from around the world, as well as desserts, ice lollies, beer, wine and coffee! All with an emphasis on quality and freshness. Besides food, we'll be serving lectures and workshops on preparing different meals as well as a rich kids program. Entertainment will be provided throughout the day by Brno's street artists.
Quality meat, fish, fruits, vegetables as well as sweets, yummies for celiacs, vegetarians and vegans from well know vendors as well as those, who can be only be met at IFF. Sitting area both indoors and outdoors. All environmentally friendly and green.

For vendors

Power socket and a shared 55m2 cold storage room for ingredients
Introducing you on Facebook and in the IFF newspaper
Possible to bring your stand and ingredients up-front
Guidance for vendors without festival experiences

For guests

Direct public transport connection from main train station
Festival newspaper and a cool button
Drinking water free for everyone
Accompanying programme for kids and adults
